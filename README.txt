Crypto Commandos is an Omni-Chain Play-to-Earn Battle Royale centered around a futuristic dystopian society at war, divided over a sacred element native to their planet Terah.

This highly immersive gaming experience features 4 unique factions consisting of 6,000 Genesis Commandos. 

Each Commando is generated with the capability to be successful in combat if the player fully understands the game theory and executes the most efficient battle tactics, regardless of their rarity.
 
Our main objective is to raise the standard of the play-to-earn industry and lead the transition from dull idle gaming to intense player-interactive combat.
